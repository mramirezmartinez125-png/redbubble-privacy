Privacy Policy

Pinterest Bulk Extractor
TL;DR: We don't collect, store, or transmit any of your personal data. Everything happens locally in your browser.

1. Information Collection

Pinterest Bulk Extractor does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information, user data, or browsing activity to any external servers.

2. Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device using Chrome's built-in storage API. This includes:

  • Your language preference (English/Spanish)
  • Extension configuration settings
  • Your encrypted license key (if purchased)
  • Saved custom extraction guides

None of this data leaves your computer.

3. Third-Party Services

The extension communicates only with:

  • Gumroad API: Only to verify your license key if you purchased the extension. No personal data is sent.

We do not track, analyze, or monitor any other communications.

4. Data Extraction

When you use the extension to extract product data from websites:

  • All extraction happens locally in your browser
  • Extracted data (titles, images, descriptions) is only stored in your browser's memory
  • CSV/Excel exports are downloaded directly to your computer
  • We never upload, transmit, or store your extracted data on any server

5. Permissions Explained

storage To save your preferences and settings locally on your device
activeTab To interact with the current webpage when you trigger the scanner
scripting To inject the content detection script that identifies products on webpages
tabs To communicate with the content script and get information about the active tab
sidePanel To provide a convenient sidebar interface for the extension

6. Your Data Rights

Since we don't collect any data, there's nothing to request, delete, or export. You have full control over all locally stored settings by:

  • Clearing extension data in Chrome settings
  • Uninstalling the extension at any time

7. Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, we will notify users through an extension update notice.
