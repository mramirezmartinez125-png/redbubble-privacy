Redbubble Auto Uploader

TL;DR: We don't collect, store, or transmit any of your personal data. Everything happens locally in your browser.

1. Information Collection

Redbubble Auto Uploader does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information, user data, or browsing activity to any external servers.

2. Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device using Chrome's built-in storage API. This includes:

  • Your language preference
  • Extension configuration settings
  • Your encrypted license key (if purchased)
  • Upload progress and automation logs

None of this data leaves your computer.

3. Third-Party Services

The extension communicates only with:

  • Redbubble.com: To automate the upload process on your behalf (requires you to be logged in)
  • Gumroad API: Only to verify your license key if you purchased the extension

We do not track, analyze, or monitor these communications.

4. Cookies

The extension uses the "cookies" permission solely to verify that you are logged into your Redbubble account before starting the automation. We do not read, modify, or share your cookies.

5. Permissions Explained

  • storage: To save your preferences locally
  • activeTab: To interact with the Redbubble page when you trigger automation
  • scripting: To fill in form fields on Redbubble automatically
  • sidePanel: To provide a convenient sidebar interface
  • cookies: To verify you're logged into Redbubble
  • clipboardWrite: To copy AI prompts to your clipboard when you click a button

6. Your Data Rights

Since we don't collect any data, there's nothing to request, delete, or export. You have full control over all locally stored settings by uninstalling the extension at any time.

7. Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, we will notify users through an extension update notice.
